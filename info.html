About Me

I am a designer with a passion for digital art. When I was 10, my brother got me Photoshop and that is where it all started. Me and my brother would create funny images and memes and fictional game cover art. Soon, we started branching off and creating YouTube icons for our channels. They weren't that good but that is where my career started. Over the years we got better and better. Soon, I started seeing if I could make money from my work as I was getting older and wanted more items. I joined multiple online groups and discord servers and I got my first sale making the Mako Shark "M" logo on the first page. I sent them my moms Paypal email and they actually sent 10 Dollars. It branched off from there and I saw a large demand for Roblox 3D renders and thumbnails. I immediately saw a boost in sales and i still make them. I am currently learning how to make 3D banners and more. Also, no one is too young to start a business.
